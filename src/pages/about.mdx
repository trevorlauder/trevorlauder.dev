I'm a Site Reliability and DevOps Engineer based in Sylvan Lake, Alberta, with more than 20 years of experience building, scaling, and maintaining reliable systems. Throughout my career, I've taken on a variety of senior technical roles, including Systems Administrator, Staff Systems Engineer, Principal Application Operations Engineer, Senior Site Reliability Engineer, and Senior Development Operations Engineer.

I've had the privilege of contributing to organizations such as [Jobber](https://www.getjobber.com/), [Guestlogix](https://www.guestlogix.com/), [SkipTheDishes](https://www.skipthedishes.com/), [Intuit](https://www.intuit.com/ca/), [TELUS](https://www.telus.com/), [LAN Solutions](https://lansolutions.com/), Wireless Networks Inc., and [Lethbridge College](https://lethbridgecollege.ca/).

Today, I work as a Senior Site Reliability Engineer at [Jobber](https://www.getjobber.com/).

Beyond my professional work, I'm passionate about automation in my home and enjoy exploring photography, astronomy, and the outdoors.
